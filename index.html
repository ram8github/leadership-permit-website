The Framework That Makes It Visible

The Leadership Permit framework reveals what traditional leadership models miss: legitimacy follows a multiplicative formula.

Weakness in any pillar undermines everything else. No amount of talent compensates for broken perception. Exceptional outcomes don't survive failure to deliver. Perfect perception doesn't outlast incompetence.

P

Perception

Do they believe in you enough to follow voluntarily?

The gap between how you intend to be perceived and how you're actually experienced. Leadership Capital that determines permission. The Erosion Paradox that degrades trust even when nothing changes.

T

Talent

Do you have the capability to guide them effectively?

The difference between expertise and bottleneck. Multiplying capability through others rather than becoming the constraint your organization routes around.

O

Outcomes

Do your results justify the trust they've placed in you?

Delivering results that strengthen organizational capacity rather than depleting the system that produces them. Sustainable performance versus borrowed time.
